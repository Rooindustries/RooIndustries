# Roo Industries 6v6 Legacy Series Rules

This is a plain Markdown backup of the public tournament rulebook currently shown on `/tourney`.

## No Warnings For Rule Breaks

Any rule break can result in immediate penalty or disqualification. Hosts and admins will rule based on proof, impact, and cooperation.

## Competitive Rules

If you join, check in, or play a map, these rules apply. Owner and designated caster rulings are final.

## Map And Hero-Ban Process

This tournament uses an OWCS-style map and hero-ban process. Map 1 is Control and the higher seed chooses the Control map and starting side. After that, the team that lost the previous map picks the next legal map, mode, and starting side. Each team bans one hero per map; teams cannot repeat their own hero ban in the same series, and both bans on a map cannot be from the same role.

## Rulebook

1. **Format**
   6v6 Overwatch. Double elimination. Bracket matches are Best of 5 and Grand Final is Best of 7 with no bracket reset.

2. **Teams**
   Captains draft teams from the approved player pool. Rosters lock at 6 starters and 2 substitutes.

3. **Roles**
   No role swaps. Players must play the role they registered, drafted, or were assigned for that series.

4. **Subs**
   Only registered substitutes can play after roster lock. Subs enter between maps, never mid-map.

5. **Check-In**
   Teams must field 6 eligible players within 10 minutes of match time or admins may call a forfeit.

6. **Maps**
   Map 1 is Control. After that, the previous map loser picks the next legal map, mode, and starting side.

7. **Hero Bans**
   Each team bans 1 hero per map. Teams cannot repeat their own ban in a series, and both bans cannot be from the same role.

8. **Conduct**
   No cheating, throwing, griefing, harassment, stream sniping, lobby leaks, alt play, impersonation, or intentional stalling.

9. **Penalties**
   Rule breaks can result in immediate penalty or disqualification. No warnings are required.

10. **Community Event Notice**
    Roo Industries runs this tournament independently. It is not endorsed, sponsored, or affiliated with Blizzard Entertainment.
